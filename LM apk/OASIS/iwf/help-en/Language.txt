282 Elves are a very withdrawn and isolated race. There are nearly no facts about how they first appeared in Quiltlands or about their culture. Elves cannot use items made by other races and never hire alien warriors. Elves' troops are not numerous, but they are very skillful in battle. Elves have excellent magic skills.
283 Dwarfs are businesslike and rich. Their underground storages are full of gold; they spend it on good weapon and armour. Not especially powerful in direct fighting, Dwarfs can very well disguise and hide their warriors. Defence is their strong point. The only race they treat friendly is Humans.
284 Orcs are an aggressive race. Their warriors possess high speed and strength when attacking. However, defence is their weak point. Orcs often attack locals, rob towns and enslave civilians, which is why other races are hostile to them. Humans are the only race that tolerates Orcs. Unlike other races, Orcs cannot have the neutral Dragons, Magicians and Banshees join them.
285 Ancient necromancers could raise people and other creatures from the dead. The resurrected were called the Undead and, as they inherited some of the necromantic craft, they have been developing and perfecting it over thousands of years. Now the armies of the Undead are numerous and strong. They possess great magic skills. Some believe that it was the Undead who were involved in the collapse of what used to be a solid world into a patchwork of disparate races. Except for Humans, all other races furiously hate the Undead.
286 Human heroes aren't too strong physically, but they are natural-born leaders. Due to their high tolerance of others, People may use magic equipment and employ units of any other race, except Elves.
